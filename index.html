About

Anebitis is the artistic identity of Ioannis Anebitis, a Greek artist working across drawing, animation, comics and experimental visual media.

His practice moves between sketchbook process, visual storytelling, symbolic image-making, moving image and research-based experimentation.

This website functions both as a portfolio and as an evolving archive of works, studies and artistic development.

He completed his studies in Audio & Visual Arts at the Ionian University, where he developed the video-art thesis project Seed Zero, a work centered on symbolic action, image construction and experimental visual language.

Current Focus

  • Sketch-based visual development
  • Animation and moving image
  • Comics and narrative experimentation
  • Video art and hybrid media
  • Artistic research and process documentation
  • Experimental media and visual symbolism
